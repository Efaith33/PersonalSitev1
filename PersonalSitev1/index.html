About Me


Hello, welcome to my portfolio. My name is Eric Faith and I am a Full Stack Web Developer. I attended and completed the Full Stack Wed Development Program from Centriq IT out of St. Louis, MO. I am from Southern Illinois and have lived here most of my life. I come from industry jobs such as Factory, railroad, ups where I grew my abilities in customer service , technical cpu/program use, team work, project managment, deadline driven , collaberation of multiple teams to complete a job or task. I enjoy working with others and have that ability to exchange ideas. At heart I'm a traveler, enjoy tinkering and always learning. Really excited to continue to learn in this career path with web developing.
